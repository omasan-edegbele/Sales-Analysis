# Sales Analysis Summary

## Overall Performance
The dataset indicates a total revenue of approximately **$340.3 million** generated during the fourth quarter of 2020.

---

## Sales by Customer Group
Sales are distributed across four customer segments: **Kids, Men, Women, and Seniors**.  
Each group exhibits distinct purchasing patterns influenced by time of day and seasonality.

- **Men’s apparel** generates the highest revenue, driven by higher unit sales and/or premium pricing.
- **Kids’ apparel** contributes lower overall revenue relative to adult segments.
- **Seniors** represent the smallest revenue contribution among the four groups.

---

## Sales by State
Sales performance varies notably across Australian states, reflecting regional differences in demand.

- States with **larger populations** and **higher consumer spending power** account for a significant portion of total revenue.
- Smaller or less urbanized states contribute comparatively lower sales figures.

---

## Monthly and Weekly Trends
The dataset covers **October, November, and December 2020**, aligning with the holiday shopping period.

- Sales show a steady increase approaching **December**, which records the highest revenue.
- Weekly sales peaks are most prominent during the holiday season, indicating heightened consumer activity.

---

## Key Takeaways
- [December](Sales_By_Month.png) is the strongest sales month, driven by holiday-related purchasing behavior.
- [Men's apparel](Sales_By_Demographic.png) is the largest revenue contributor across customer segments. Meanwhile, **Seniors** consistently generate the lowest sales, highlighting potential opportunities for targeted sales programs.
- [Victoria](Sales_By_State.png) is the state with the most sales highlighting its dense population followed by New South Wales.
